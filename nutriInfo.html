1. What is the purpose of the Smart Food Scanner app?
⇒ The Smart Food Scanner app helps users make informed decisions by scanning food barcodes to provide detailed health insights, including suitability for specific dietary needs like diabetes, and information on the environmental impact of the products.

2. How does the app work?
⇒ Simply scan the barcode of a food product using the app. It will instantly retrieve and display important information about the product, including health suitability, environmental impact, and other relevant details.

3. What kind of health information does the app provide?
⇒ The app provides insights on whether a product is suitable for specific dietary needs, such as for people with diabetes. It may also include nutritional information like sugar content and general health impact.

4. How does the app assess the environmental impact of a product?
⇒ The app evaluates the environmental impact based on available data about the product’s production, packaging, and other factors. This helps users understand the ecological footprint of their purchases.

5. Is the app free to use?
⇒ The app will be available for free with basic features.

6. Can I use the app offline?
⇒ Barcode scanning requires an internet connection to retrieve detailed product information.

7. What types of products can be scanned with the app?
⇒ The app is designed to work with a wide range of food products with barcodes. It may not be compatible with products without barcodes or those with unreadable codes.

8. How accurate is the information provided by the app?
⇒ The app relies on the accuracy of the product data available through its database. We strive to provide reliable information, but users should also verify important health-related details with other trusted sources.

9. How can I provide feedback or report issues with the app?
⇒ You can provide feedback or report issues through the app’s support section or by contacting us directly at Support Link.

10. Will my data be secure?
⇒ Yes, we prioritize user privacy and data security. Your personal data will be protected according to our privacy policy, and we will not share your information with third parties without your consent.
